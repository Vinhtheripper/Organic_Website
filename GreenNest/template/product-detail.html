Spanish Garbanzo Beans with Aioli Toasts & Romesco Sauce

35 minutes 640 calories 2 servings
$12.99

Savor the robust flavors of Spain with our delicious garbanzo bean stew. This hearty dish features protein-rich chickpeas simmered with aromatic vegetables and smoky paprika, served alongside crusty toasts spread with creamy aioli and topped with a vibrant romesco sauce. A perfect balance of textures and tastes!

Nutrition Highlights

High Protein
Heart Healthy
High Fiber
Ingredients
Cooking Instructions
Nutrition Facts

In Your Box

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 baguette
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ¬º oz fresh parsley
  • 1 yellow onion
  • ¬º cup almonds
  • 2 tomatoes

What You'll Need

  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Paprika
  • Red wine vinegar
1

Prep the Vegetables

Preheat the oven to 425¬∞F. Peel and small dice the onion. Mince the garlic. Small dice the tomatoes. Core, deseed, and small dice the bell pepper. Roughly chop the parsley leaves.

2

Make the Romesco Sauce

In a food processor, combine the almonds, half the garlic, 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp red wine vinegar, and a pinch of salt. Process until smooth, adding water if needed to reach desired consistency.

3

Cook the Garbanzo Beans

Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 4-5 minutes until softened. Add the remaining garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Add the diced tomatoes, bell pepper, drained chickpeas, 1 tsp paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

4

Make the Aioli & Toast

Slice the baguette on a diagonal into 1-inch thick pieces. Place on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and toast in the oven for 5-7 minutes until golden. Meanwhile, mix 3 tbsp vegan mayonnaise with 1 minced garlic clove and a squeeze of lemon juice for the aioli.

5

Serve

Spread the aioli on the toasted baguette slices. Spoon the garbanzo bean mixture into bowls, garnish with chopped parsley, and serve with the aioli toasts and romesco sauce on the side.
